Delivery & Returns

We deliver premium electronics across Kenya. Delivery costs and timelines are confirmed via WhatsApp after you place your order.

Delivery

  • Nairobi & environs: Same-day or next-day delivery available for in-stock items.
  • Countrywide: We ship to all major towns in Kenya. Delivery typically takes 2–5 business days.
  • Large appliances (fridges, washing machines, ACs): Delivery fee depends on location and may include stair carry or installation — we'll quote this on WhatsApp.
  • Free delivery may apply on select promotions — ask us on WhatsApp for current offers.

Installation

We offer installation services for air conditioners, washing machines, and wall-mounted TVs in select areas. Mention this when placing your order and we'll arrange a technician.

Returns & Exchanges

  • 7-day return policy on unused items in original packaging with all accessories.
  • Items must be in resalable condition — no damage, scratches, or missing parts.
  • Defective products under warranty will be repaired or replaced per manufacturer policy.
  • To initiate a return, contact us on WhatsApp with your order details and reason.

Warranty

All products are 100% genuine and come with official manufacturer warranty. Warranty period varies by product and brand — we'll confirm details at the time of purchase.
