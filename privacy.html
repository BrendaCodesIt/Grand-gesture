Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. Grand Gesture respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name and phone number (via checkout or WhatsApp)
  • Delivery address or location
  • Order details (products, quantities, prices)
  • Browser data stored locally (cart and wishlist items in your device)

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To arrange delivery and communicate about your purchase
  • To respond to enquiries and provide customer support
  • To improve our products and services

Data Storage

Cart and wishlist data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage — it is not sent to our servers unless you submit an order via WhatsApp. Order information shared on WhatsApp is handled according to WhatsApp's privacy policy.
